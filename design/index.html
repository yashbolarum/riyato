Motion

Brand motion and globe asset.

Use the globe for hero treatments and motion cards. Keep animation subtle and let gold remain the only active color.

Motion rules

Keep movement restrained, smooth, and directional. The goal is to make the brand feel precise, not noisy.

  • Reveal text with a short rise-up and fade-in, never a bounce.
  • Use the globe as a premium anchor, not a decorative background loop.
  • Let gold pulses communicate emphasis, not urgency.
  • On reduced-motion devices, keep the page stable and skip reveal choreography.
Usage

How to keep it on-brand.

These notes are here to save the editor from guesswork when a frame needs a quick branded treatment.

Do

Use the gold monogram for tiny surfaces Tabs, avatar corners, lower-thirds, and any 16 to 32 pixel usage.
Keep strong contrast Gold on navy or navy on cloud gives the identity room to breathe.
Write in short, direct sentences The tone is confident and plainspoken, not clever for its own sake.

Don't

Stretch or rotate the mark Keep the geometry intact so the globe reads immediately.
Replace gold with another accent The brand depends on one clear highlight color.
Use low-contrast placements Avoid putting the identity on noisy or near-tone backgrounds.
